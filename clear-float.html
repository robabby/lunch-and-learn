Clear & Float

Floating and clearing elements is a very popular way to manipulate content, and the layout of a page. They are also some of the most misunderstood, and mis-used concepts in web development.

Learning how and when to float or clear an element to get your layout to work the way you are wanting to can take a lot of time and patience. You can lose a lot of hours trying to hammer that floated image into the proper place, and once you do, your paragraph overflows and your back at the drawing board.

Float

With CSS float, an element can be pushed to the left or right, allowing other elements to wrap around it.

Float is very often used for images, but it is also useful when working with layouts.
